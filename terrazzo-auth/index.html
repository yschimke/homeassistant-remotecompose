Terrazzo

A small Android app that talks to a user's own Home Assistant instance. It scans the LAN for HA, asks you to sign in via the normal HA web flow, picks up your dashboards, and lets you install up to five HA cards as live-updating Android widgets.

This page exists as the app's IndieAuth client_id. See github.com/yschimke/homeassistant-remotecompose.
